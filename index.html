A little about me

My name is Sam Canoe, I am 30 years old, I am currently employed with one of Louisiana's largest security companies while I am waiting for the right opportunity to chase my dreams. I recently got married in September to my beautiful wife and I have three wonderful stepchildren. We settled down on top of the hill overlooking an 18 acre pond ,in a little town in Grant Parish,La which has giving me an edge of being able to fish everyday, even if I'm not out fishing a tournament. I was introduced to fishing at a very young age through my dad and stepdad. Our main fishing spot was Toledo Bend, which is the best lake in the country right now in my opinion, ever since then I was hooked. I received my first boat when I turned 16 and I have been fishing for bass recreationally and competitively since then.
