A little about me

Sam was named after a Sam from Sam and Kat on Disney Channel. He was also given the name of a small water vessel. Having the last name of Canoe meant that he was destined to do something on the water. He tried water polo but after many attempts he couldnt get the rules down. He also tried to make the Olympics in swimming but kept getting beaten by Michael Phelps. He decided fishing was something he could do and thus began his career.

After placing in the top 10 at the FLW in 2000, he began to party and left the fishing world until 2012. He began to make a strong presence in the tournament scene and in 2014 placed in the top 10 in the FLW for a second time. Also during this time he won the Bassmaster Classic every year from 2012 to 2014.
